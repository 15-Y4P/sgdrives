Which category is my car?

Cat A: non-fully-electric cars up to 1,600cc and 97kW (130bhp), EVs up to 110kW. Cat B: above those thresholds. Cat E: open category, usable for any vehicle.

What is PQP?

The Prevailing Quota Premium — a 3-month moving average of winning premiums, payable to renew an existing COE instead of bidding.
